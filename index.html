COFFEE HOUSE

Fresh food

Though we are primarily a coffee house, we also serve food. Our cantina is always open during working hours and is full with fresh ingredients, so have no fear of going hungry at our place! Come on in and enjoy the daily specialties prepared by our chefs!

Free WiFi

This goes without saying, but here at CoffeeHouse, wifi is free, so have no fear of coming in alone and enjoying a delicious cup of coffee :)

did someone say "coffee"

We have worked very hard to bring the very best coffee beans from around the world to our house, and thus, to you. These are only some of the cofee we serve: Koa Coffee from Hawaii, Blue Mountain Coffee from Jamaica, Volcanica Coffee from Kenya, we also make coffee out of Peaberry Beans from Tanzania and Sumatra Mandheling Beans from Indonesia. Come to our small house if you want to enjoy those and discover more delicious secrets that we are keeping just for you!
